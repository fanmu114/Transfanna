ABOUT ME

I am Zeling, a freelance translator who has worked as an English>Chinese Native translator for more than seven years. Graduating from Shanghai International Studies University, I hold a Master’s Degree in English Language and Literature (Subfield: Translation/Interpretation).

Before becoming a freelancer, I worked in three industries— game development, fashion retail, and real estate development which helped me understand these industries by and large. Also, I have worked on many documents regarding business management, marketing, e-commerce, legal, financial, and IT/technology, etc..

Currently, I am a Pro translator (English<>Chinese) with Lionbridge Gengo.
